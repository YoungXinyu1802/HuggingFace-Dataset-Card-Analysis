This is the summarization datasets collected by TextBox, including:
- CNN/Daily Mail (cnndm)
- XSum (xsum)
- SAMSum (samsum)
- WLE (wle)
- Newsroom (nr)
- WikiHow (wikihow)
- MicroSoft News (msn)
- MediaSum (mediasum)
- English Gigaword (eg).

The detail and leaderboard of each dataset can be found in [TextBox page](https://github.com/RUCAIBox/TextBox#dataset).
