### Dataset Summary

This dataset contains the list of Top 100 Richest People in the World

Column Information:- 

- Name - Person Name
- NetWorth - His/Her Networth
- Age - Person Age
- Country - The country person belongs to
- Source - Information Source
- Industry - Expertise Domain
