This is the data-to-text generation datasets collected by TextBox, including:
- WebNLG v2.1 (webnlg)
- WebNLG v3.0 (webnlg2)
- WikiBio (wikibio)
- E2E (e2e)
- DART (dart)
- ToTTo (totto)
- ENT-DESC (ent)
- AGENDA (agenda)
- GenWiki (genwiki)
- TEKGEN (tekgen)
- LogicNLG (logicnlg)
- WikiTableT (wikit)
- WEATHERGOV (wg).

The detail and leaderboard of each dataset can be found in [TextBox page](https://github.com/RUCAIBox/TextBox#dataset).
