This is the question generation datasets collected by TextBox, including:
- SQuAD (squadqg)
- CoQA (coqaqg)
- NewsQA (newsqa)
- HotpotQA (hotpotqa)
- MS MARCO (marco)
- MSQG (msqg)
- NarrativeQA (nqa)
- QuAC (quac).

The detail and leaderboard of each dataset can be found in [TextBox page](https://github.com/RUCAIBox/TextBox#dataset).
